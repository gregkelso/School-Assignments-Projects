Welcome to Penumbra by Greg Kelso

The goal of this game is to survive as long as possible. You won't win. This game is controlled with the keyboard only. Due to the weird physics engine, make sure you lightly tap the movement and jumping keys, instead of holding them down like in other video games. You will have time to practice before the challenge begins. After reading the controls below, close this menu and press 'B' on your keyboard to begin the game. Good luck.


Begin B
Movement WASD
Jumping J
Permanent Mute M
